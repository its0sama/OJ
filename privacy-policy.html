Privacy Policy

Overview

This site is a static, client-side personality assessment. Your responses are processed in your browser. No user answers are transmitted to a server.

Data Collection

  • No analytics or tracking cookies are included by default.

Third-Party Services

The site may load fonts from external providers (e.g., Google Fonts). Those providers may receive standard web requests (such as font loading), but your assessment answers are not sent to them.
